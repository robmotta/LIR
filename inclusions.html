Why do we do it?

When it comes to remote planning it is sometimes complicated to actually find out of all the options available other than the ones the wedding planner you hired is showing you out of convenience. We decided to create a hub of information and display all options available in the local market (or all the ones we have been able to record) as well as some preset themes that might ease your organization if you want to go straight to the point. Love in Roatan looks to make your experience of organizing and celebrating your special day as simple and fast as possible without losing the personal touch of your own personal planner.

How It Works

It is a simple process, pick everything you need for your event, fill in a few forms regarding some other important details and then leave the rest to us.

Choose your Location

The first step is to chose from out list of available locations, you can review every detail each venue includes and does not includes, every venue is different, so make sure you read all the decription

Pick Your theme or customize your event

Choose from our list of items everything that you need for your event, if you do not find something you might have interest off, you can Contact us to see if we can find it outside our inventory list. Once you finish selecting your items you will have delivered an instant quote of everything to your email and our online wedding pre-planner will contact you to polish the edges.

Wait for it...

Close the deal by making the first deposit and saving the date with us. You can schedule a test-drive of our services and visit us prior your wedding for an additional fee, special rates can be applied to you and your friends visiting to test the food and location of your prefference.
