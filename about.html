Who are we?

Love In Roatan is an Online wedding & event planning agency, we started out by putting together the expertise of the founders, each one in their own field with the skills and energy combined, this idea came up, Roatan Has alot of potential and we want to make sure you see it. The experience on our team goes back as much as fifteen years of experience in event and wedding planning as well as businesses related to public entretainment such as restaurants and bars. We know what our clients are looking for and if we happen to find any challenge we sure will make sure you are satisfied. We take pride in our event planning skills and photography to make you live the experience and make sure it is recorded.

Why do we do it?

When it comes to remote planning it is sometimes complicated to actually find out of all the options available other than the ones the wedding planner you hired is showing you out of convenience. We decided to create a hub of information and display all options available in the market (or all the ones we know of) as well as some preset themes that might ease your organization if you want to go straight to the point. Love in Roatan looks to make your experience of organizing and celebrating your special day as simple and fast as possible without losing the personal touch of your own personal planner.

If you are someone that could help us expand the list of options available to everyone who wants to get married or celebrate their event, please drop us a line and we will make sure your props are displayed in our list
