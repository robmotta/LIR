Who are we?

Love In Roatan is an online wedding & event planning agency. We started out by putting together the expertise of the founders. With the expertise of each one in their own field, this idea came up. Roatan has a lot of potential and we want to make sure you see it. The experience on our team goes back to fifteen years of event and wedding planning as well as catering and entretainment. We take pride in our event planning skills and photography to make you live the experience and make sure it is recorded.

We will make your ideas come true.

Although we do have an online inventory available for you to put together your own event, we are are constantly on the lookout of new items and props. If you feel our inventory doesn't have what you need, talk to our wedding pre-planner to discuss your ideas and we will try our best to find what you want.

When it comes to events, anything is possible. Our team is prepared to give their utter best in the planification and execution of the event.

Why do we do it?

When it comes to remote planning it is sometimes complicated to actually find out of all the options available other than the ones the wedding planner you hired is showing you out of convenience. We decided to create a hub of information and display all options available in the local market (or all the ones we have been able to record) as well as some preset themes that might ease your organization if you want to go straight to the point. Love in Roatan aims to make your experience of organizing and celebrating your special day as simple and fast as possible without losing the personal touch.

If you are someone that has services or products to offer contact us and we will surely get in touch. The more the merrier!

How It Works

It is a simple process, pick everything you need for your event, fill in a few forms regarding some other important details and then leave the rest to us.

Choose your Location

The first step is to chose from our list of available locations. You can review the venue's details by visiting our location list. Make sure you read all the decription and chose theone that suits you the best.

Pick Your theme or customize your event

Chose everything you need from our list of items. if you do not find something you might have interest on, you can let us know to see if we can find it outside our inventory list. Once you finish selecting your items you will receive an instant quote of everything you selected to your email. One of our online wedding pre-planners will contact you to polish the edges.

Wait for it...

Close the deal by making the first deposit and saving the date with us. You can schedule a test-drive of our services and visit us prior your wedding for an additional fee, special rates can be applied to you and your friends visiting to test the food and location of your prefference.

Making everything easier

Our Objective is for you to find everything you need in Roatan for your wedding or party, if you think we are missing something, don't forget to mention it.
